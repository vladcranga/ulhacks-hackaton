The best productivity software and tips

EduHub Team, Aug 14, 2021

If you've ever found yourself procrastinating when you said you'd do something productive, don't worry - you're not alone, and there are solutions as well. Some tips and free apps can help you right away!

Some tips include:

  • Write down the tasks you need to do
  • Start as early as possible
  • Focus on one task at a time
  • Set deadlines
  • Start slowly at first, then do more
  • Learn specific tricks to do the tasks faster
  • Start with the most difficult task
  • Plan for the future
  • Exercise
  • Get enough sleep
  • Review your week
  • Get rid of distractions
  • Take breaks from time to time

If you feel like there's so much to do with your limited resources, there are a lot of free apps that can facilitate the planning process for you:

  • Google Calendar
  • One Calendar
  • Obsidian (note taking)
  • Evernote (note taking)
  • ClickUp (to do list)
  • Todoist (to do list)
  • Notion (note taking and planning)
  • TickTick (productivity 'pomos')
